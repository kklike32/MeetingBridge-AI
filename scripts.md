# MeetingBridge AI Live Demo Script

## One-Minute Talk Track

**0:00-0:10 - Open**

"MeetingBridge AI helps people understand meetings, not just read transcripts. A transcript can capture every word and still leave a new hire, intern, non-native English speaker, or accessibility user stuck on acronyms like GTM, ARR, PLG, and churn."

**0:10-0:20 - What It Does**

"This app starts from real meeting audio. I can record through the browser or upload a short clip. Then it transcribes locally, explains jargon with a local LLM, and keeps a human reviewer in control."

**0:20-0:30 - Record Or Upload**

"I will use the demo sentence now: Let's revisit our GTM motion before Q3 and improve ARR through our PLG initiative while reducing churn across enterprise accounts."

**0:30-0:40 - Transcript And AI**

"The audio becomes a local ASR transcript first. If an acronym is misheard, I correct it here. Now I click Analyze locally, which sends the corrected transcript to the selected local model."

**0:40-0:50 - Human Review**

"The AI suggests simplifications and glossary explanations, but nothing is final yet. I can approve GTM, edit ARR to be clearer, and reject anything ambiguous."

**0:50-1:00 - Close**

"The final notes use only the human-approved glossary. Participants get a plain-English summary, reviewed terms, action items, and an audit trail. Real audio in, human-approved understanding out."
